    Tip(s)

    Insured must be 18-65 years old.

    All beneficiaries below 18 years old must have a legal guardian or trustee.

    More Tips

    If Insured is MARRIED:

    • Dependents must be Spouse and Child/ren ONLY.
    • Spouse must be 18-65 years old.
    • Children must be 1-21 years old.
    • Unmarried child/ren (up to 3).

    If Insured is SINGLE:

    • Dependents must be Parent/s and Sibling/s ONLY.
    • Parents must be immediate parents and NOT in-laws.
    • Parent/s must be 18-65 years old.
    • Sibling/s must not be married (up to 3).
    • Sibling/s must be 1-21 years old.

    If Insured is WIDOWED/ANNULED:

    • Dependents must be Child/ren ONLY.
    • Child/ren must be 1-21 years old.
    • Unmarried child/ren (up to 3).

    If Insured is SINGLE PARENT:

    • Dependents must be Parents and Child/ren ONLY.
    • Child/ren must be 1-21 years old.
    • Unmarried child/ren (up to 3).

    Insured's beneficiary are the ones who can claim the benefits in case you or a family member die or are disabled in the accident.

    • Beneficiary must be Immediate Family Members ONLY.
